What is LignumAI?

LignumAI is an AI model built to read and understand technical drawings. Just as ChatGPT understands language, LignumAI interprets architectural blueprints, engineering sketches, and schematic plans. It identifies elements, structures, annotations, and context — transforming static drawings into usable, intelligent data.

Why LignumAI?

Seeing beyond the lines. LignumAI helps teams save time, reduce errors, digitize archives, and unlock insights hidden in technical visuals — without manual interpretation.
